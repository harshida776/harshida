MESCE

MESCE is the MES College of Engineering, a self-financing institution in Kuttippuram, Kerala,
established in 1994 with a Muslim minority status to provide quality technical education and empower
the community. Affiliated with the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and approved by AICTE and CoA,
it offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and PhD programs in various engineering disciplines, along with
Architecture, MCA, and MBA programs, and has an NBA accreditation
